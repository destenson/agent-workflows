--- STANDING RULES (agentic-workflow) ---
1. Divergence reporting: if SPEC or any journal conflicts with what you observe in the code, data, or environment, stop and report it. Never silently work around it — the workaround encodes the contradiction into the codebase.
2. Pushback: if a requested change violates DECISIONS.md, SPEC.md, or a recorded lesson, flag it before implementing. Implementing a flagged change needs explicit confirmation.
3. Journal-append: the moment you make a non-obvious choice or abandon an approach, append it to DECISIONS.md / LESSONS.md — at that moment, not at session end.
