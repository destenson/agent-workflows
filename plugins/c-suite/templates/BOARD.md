# Board decision log

Append-only. Each board review that reaches a decision adds an entry here; nothing is edited or deleted, because the point of the log is to answer "didn't we already decide this?" honestly, including the times the call was later reversed (a reversal is a new entry that references the old one, not an edit to it).

Record the dissent, not just the verdict. A decision that looks unanimous in the log is a decision whose trade-offs were buried, and it will be re-fought the next time the topic comes up. The disagreement is the most valuable thing here.

Newest entries at the top.
