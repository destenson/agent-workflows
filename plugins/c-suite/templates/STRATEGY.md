# Strategy

The current direction and its measurable objectives. Every officer review is implicitly scored against this document: a proposal that advances no stated objective is a finding, not a detail. Keep this short and current — a strategy nobody can recite is not steering anything. Revisit it on a fixed cadence (each quarter is a reasonable default) and record material changes in `BOARD.md`.
