# Results — {project title}

What the research has established, synthesized against the pre-committed criteria — not raw output. Includes the negative results: falsified hypotheses and abandoned approaches are recorded here with the same weight as positive findings, because they carry the most reuse value and are the most often dropped.

The `results-memo`, `negative-results`, and `decision-memo` skills produce the detailed versions; this artifact holds the durable summary.

## {date} — {finding title}
- **Claim**: what is now believed, stated precisely
- **Evidence**: which experiment(s) support it, measured against the pre-committed bar (link to the experiment / results memo)
- **Verdict vs. criteria**: did it meet the bar set in the proposal? supported · not supported · inconclusive
- **Decision**: what this implies — ship / iterate / kill, and what it spawns next
