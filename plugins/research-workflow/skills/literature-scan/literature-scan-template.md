## Failure modes in prior work

What has been tried, failed, and why. This is often the most important section and the most commonly omitted. A proposal that ignores known failure modes of prior approaches is likely to reproduce them.

## Gap

What the prior work does not address, or addresses poorly, that the current problem requires. This is the justification for a new proposal. State it as a specific absence, not as a vague inadequacy.
