Before this session ends, record what a future session (or a colleague at 3am) would otherwise have to reconstruct.

- CHANGELOG.md: append every change made to a live system this session — host, what changed, why. This is the record that makes "it worked last week" answerable.
- INCIDENTS.md: for any incident worked this session, append the resolution and root cause (or, if unresolved, the current state and what to try next).
- RUNBOOKS.md: if a procedure was worked out this session that will be needed again, capture it as a runbook.
- FLEET.md: if anything about a host's access, role, or constraints changed or was discovered, update the map.

Record in proportion to what was done and learned, not the size of the session. A read-only investigation that changed nothing and resolved nothing may have nothing to record — if so, say "nothing to record" explicitly. Declaring empty is the normal, frictionless outcome; do not invent filler.
